Welcome to D&D Portal

Dungeons & Dragons is a fantasy tabletop role-playing game (RPG). It is normally played indoors with the participants seated around a tabletop. Typically, each player controls only a single character, which represents an individual in a fictional setting. This new kind of game has become immensely popular over the years, and D&D has grown to include many new ways to vividly experience worlds of heroic fantasy.
